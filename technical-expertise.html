Technical Expertise Built for Standards-Compliant Engineering

Our engineering strength spans process design, simulation tools, heat and material balance development, equipment sizing, utility systems, and process safety — governed throughout by international codes and standards.

Process Design

Development of process flow diagrams, flow schemes, and control philosophies for grassroots and revamp projects across all industry sectors we serve.

Simulation Tools

Steady-state and dynamic simulation for process validation, plant debottlenecking, and operability and control system design.

Heat & Material Balance

Rigorous heat and material balance development underpinning equipment sizing, utility loads, and energy efficiency assessments.

Equipment Sizing

Sizing of vessels, exchangers, pumps, compressors, and reactors in accordance with process requirements and applicable mechanical codes.

Utility Systems

Design and integration of steam, power, cooling water, compressed air, and fuel gas systems supporting reliable plant operation.

Process Safety

Hazard identification, process safety studies, and relief and flare system design to protect personnel, assets, and the environment.

Piping & Instrumentation

Development of piping and instrumentation diagrams, line lists, and control loop specifications for detailed engineering packages.

Engineering Documentation

Disciplined document control and issue management for FEED and detailed engineering deliverables, maintaining full technical traceability.

International Codes & Standards

Engineering executed and verified against applicable international codes and standards for every discipline and jurisdiction.

Codes & Standards Compliance

Engineering Aligned With API, ASME, ISO, IEC, and NFPA

Standards compliance is embedded into our engineering workflow, not applied as an afterthought. Our deliverables are prepared with direct reference to the following code bodies, alongside client-specific engineering specifications.

API

American Petroleum Institute standards for petroleum and petrochemical equipment, piping, and facility design.

ASME

Mechanical design, pressure vessel, and piping code compliance across process and utility equipment.

ISO

Quality, environmental, and management system standards applied across engineering and documentation processes.

IEC

Electrical and instrumentation standards governing control systems, hazardous area classification, and equipment selection.

NFPA

Fire protection and process safety codes referenced in hazard studies and facility layout design.

Client Specifications

Project-specific engineering standards and specifications integrated alongside international codes.

Quality Systems

Structured quality management supporting design verification, review, and approval workflows.

Industrial Compliance

Engineering practices aligned with the safety and compliance expectations of regulators and operators.
