Energy Transition Consultancy for Industrial Decarbonization

Supporting industrial decarbonization and sustainable energy transition projects globally — BNKM International brings process engineering rigor to green ammonia, CCUS, SAF, and low-carbon fuels initiatives.

A Partner for Sustainable Industrial Transformation

Applying Proven Process Engineering Discipline to Low-Carbon Projects

The energy transition demands the same technical rigor as conventional hydrocarbons and chemicals engineering — validated process design, robust heat and material balance, and disciplined equipment sizing — applied to new process chemistries and integration challenges. BNKM International's energy transition consultancy combines this engineering discipline with a clear understanding of the commercial and regulatory drivers shaping green ammonia, CCUS, and SAF projects.

Our teams support developers, EPC contractors, and industrial operators from early technical feasibility studies through FEED engineering and detailed engineering solutions, with process safety and standards compliance embedded throughout.

Sustainability Commitment

Supporting Industrial Decarbonization and Sustainable Energy Transition Projects Globally

Every engagement — from a carbon reduction study on an operating refinery to a green ammonia FEED package — is designed to advance our clients' decarbonization objectives without compromising process safety or plant reliability.

Energy Transition Capabilities

Low-Carbon Engineering Solutions Across the Value Chain

Green Ammonia Engineering

Process design and integration engineering for renewable hydrogen-to-ammonia synthesis schemes, including variable power integration and synthesis loop optimization.

CCUS Consultancy

Capture technology screening, process integration, and transport and storage interface engineering for carbon capture, utilization and storage projects.

SAF Engineering

Process engineering for sustainable aviation fuel production routes, covering feedstock handling, hydroprocessing, and blending and logistics infrastructure.

Carbon Reduction Studies

Site-wide carbon reduction studies and abatement roadmaps, quantifying emissions reduction potential and associated capital and operating implications.

Energy Efficiency

Energy efficiency studies and utility integration to reduce specific energy consumption across process and utility systems.

Low-Carbon Fuels

Process engineering support for e-fuels, hydrogen derivatives, and other low-carbon fuel pathways moving from pilot to commercial scale.

Industrial Decarbonization

Roadmap development and engineering support to help operating assets transition toward lower-carbon production pathways.

Sustainable Energy Transition Projects

Technical feasibility studies and project engineering support for emerging low-carbon energy infrastructure and industrial electrification schemes.

"Green ammonia, CCUS, and SAF projects succeed or fail on process engineering fundamentals — heat and material balance, utility integration, and process safety — exactly the disciplines our teams apply every day across hydrocarbons and chemicals."
