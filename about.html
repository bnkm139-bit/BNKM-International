Engineering Integrity for Hydrocarbons, Chemicals, Fertilizers & Energy Transition Projects

BNKM International is a process engineering and project consultancy company focused on delivering technically sound, standards-compliant, and sustainable engineering solutions to industrial clients worldwide.

Who We Are

A Process Engineering Consultancy Built for Complex Industrial Projects

BNKM International operates as a specialist process engineering consultancy, supporting owner-operators, EPC contractors, and energy transition developers across hydrocarbons, chemicals, fertilizers, and low-carbon energy sectors. Our engineering practice is structured around technical feasibility studies, process design, FEED engineering, and detailed engineering solutions — delivered by multi-disciplinary teams who understand the operating realities of refineries, petrochemical complexes, and fertilizer plants.

We approach every assignment with the same principle: engineering deliverables must be technically defensible, safe, and compliant with the codes and standards our clients are held to — API, ASME, ISO, IEC, and NFPA among them. This discipline extends to our growing energy transition portfolio, where we apply proven process engineering methods to green ammonia, CCUS, and sustainable aviation fuel projects.
