Process Engineering Consultancy Services for Industrial & Energy Transition Projects

From technical feasibility studies to detailed engineering solutions, BNKM International supports the full engineering lifecycle with process design, simulation, and standards-compliant documentation.

Engineering Delivery

Field-Proven Methodology Behind Every Engineering Deliverable

Our process engineering consultancy services are built on rigorous simulation, disciplined equipment sizing, and hands-on plant experience — so every FEED engineering and detailed engineering solutions package we issue holds up under construction and operating conditions.

Process Engineering Consultancy

Core process design services covering flow schemes, heat and material balance, and equipment sizing for grassroots and revamp projects across hydrocarbons, chemicals, and fertilizers.

FEED Engineering

Front-end engineering design packages that establish process basis, equipment lists, and cost-defining deliverables ahead of project sanction.

Detailed Engineering Solutions

Multi-discipline detailed engineering — process, piping and instrumentation, mechanical, and electrical — issued for construction and procurement.

Refinery Engineering Services

Engineering support for crude, conversion, and treating units, including revamp studies, capacity enhancement, and reliability improvement.

Process Simulation & Optimization

Steady-state process simulation to validate process design, identify plant debottlenecking opportunities, and support process optimization initiatives.

Dynamic Simulation

Dynamic simulation studies for startup, shutdown, and transient operability analysis, supporting control system design and process safety reviews.

Utility Integration

Integration of steam, power, cooling, and fuel systems with process units to optimize overall plant energy balance and reliability.

Energy Efficiency Studies

Energy efficiency assessments identifying heat recovery, process integration, and utility optimization opportunities to reduce specific energy consumption.

Carbon Reduction Studies

Carbon reduction studies and abatement roadmaps for existing operating assets, aligned with clients' industrial decarbonization targets.

Technical Feasibility Studies

Independent technical feasibility studies to screen process options, assess technology readiness, and support investment decision-making.

Project Engineering Support

Owner's engineer and project engineering support through procurement, construction, and commissioning phases, protecting technical intent.

Process Safety & Standards Compliance

Process safety studies and standards compliance verification against API, ASME, ISO, IEC, and NFPA requirements throughout project execution.
